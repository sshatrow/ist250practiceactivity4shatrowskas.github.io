About Us

Our Origin Story

Founded in 1999 by an eccentric rail enthusiast, Railroad St Game Studios has been fulfilling the desires of gamers. Our first project, 2001's "Subway Maker", was a modest success and ever since we have strived to be bigger and getter with each new game. We even branched out into different genres such as in 2009's horror/sim crossover game "Haunted Railroad Simulator 2009" and 2011's "Pinball Express" where players had to drive the train using a pinball table.
