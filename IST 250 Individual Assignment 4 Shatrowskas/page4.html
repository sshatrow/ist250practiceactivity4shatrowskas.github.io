Photo of Bruce from my copy of "Crazy Nights."

Replacing Mark in 1984, Bruce stuck around for awhile, playing with the band until 1996. He played on classics like "Tears are Falling" and "Crazy Crazy Nights." Some albums that he featured on include:

  • Asylum
  • Crazy Nights
  • Hot in the Shade

Bruce is well loved by the fans and regarded as a very professional person to work with.
