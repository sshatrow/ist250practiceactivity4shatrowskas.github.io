Also known as "Space Ace", Ace Frehley was Kiss' original lead guitarist. Some of the classic albums he played on include:

  • Dressed to Kill
  • Destroyer
  • Dynasty

He played with the band from its founding in 1973 until 1982, when he quit due to not liking the direction the band was going in. He rejoined for a brief period from 1996 to 2002, before once again quitting the band. Ace is well loved by the fans and is deeply missed.
